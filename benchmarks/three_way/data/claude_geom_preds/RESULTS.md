## Conclusion

Even with maximum reasoning effort, the frontier model cannot match a dedicated
segmentation model (Mask2Former) on pixel-precise geometry. The hybrid pipeline
wins decisively on geometry while matching on semantics — validating the thesis
that each component should handle its own role.
